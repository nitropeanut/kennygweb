who is behind all this

I live to take great photos

Photo's are my passion, I live to take great photos at any occasion. This has brought me into the profession with photos shown the portfolio below. Its not just about the photos, for me its bring out great emotions out of the photos taken. A smile out of the photos I have taken is valued so dearly. My journey in photography started off with a $100 camera body I bought from Gumtree and a few scrappy film lenses from ebay. I donated the $100 camera to Salvo's in hope that someone will start their photography journey the same way I did. My interest started in music photography as I love bands and indie music myself. I then gained interest in night photography and landscapes and portraits.
